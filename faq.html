Frequently Asked Questions

Below are some of the questions customers commonly ask about The Munoosh Shop.

Where are you located?

We are located at 54 Saywell Rd, Macquarie Fields NSW 2564.

What are your opening hours?

We are open from 8:30am to 9:00pm Monday to Friday and 8:30am to 9:30pm on Saturday and Sunday.

Do you offer takeaway?

Yes. Customers can order takeaway and collect their food from our restaurant.

What food do you serve?

We serve Lebanese food including traditional manoosh, pizzas, wraps, burgers, sides and drinks.
